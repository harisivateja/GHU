Key Data Insights

Analysis of our dashboard data reveals critical patterns driving student retention and success:

  • Dropout Risk: 29.1% of students (291 out of 1,000) are currently identified as at-risk based on multi-factor assessment.
  • Departmental Performance: Engineering and Computer Science departments outperform Psychology and Mathematics in average GPA, indicating potential curriculum or resource allocation differences.
  • Financial Stress Impact: Students with debt exceeding $15,000 face double the dropout risk compared to those with lower balances, highlighting the critical need for targeted financial aid.
  • Scholarship Effectiveness: Scholarships increase student retention rates by 33 percentage points, demonstrating significant ROI on financial aid investments.
  • Engagement Threshold: Engagement scores below 50 are strongly predictive of GPA decline, identifying a critical early warning indicator.
  • Housing Advantage: On-campus students exhibit an 18% higher retention rate than off-campus students, suggesting the value of campus community integration.
  • Long-Term Improvement: Horizon State University's 5-year retention rate has improved from 78% to 90%, demonstrating sustained progress in student success initiatives.
